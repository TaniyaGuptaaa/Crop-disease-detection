Our mission is to enhance apple farming by leveraging cutting-edge technology to detect diseases in apple crops early and accurately. Utilizing the power of deep learning,LLMs and the ResNet-50 model, our project aims to identify some apple diseases, ensuring that farmers can take timely actions to protect their crops and maximize yield. Why This Project? Apple farming is a critical sector of agriculture, and diseases can significantly impact crop quality and yield. Traditional methods of disease detection are often time-consuming and require expert knowledge. Our project aims to simplify this process by providing an automated, reliable, and quick solution for identifying apple diseases. How It Works Data Collection: We gather a vast dataset of apple images, including healthy apples and those affected by various diseases. Model Training: Using the ResNet-50 architecture, a deep convolutional neural network, we train our model to recognize patterns and features associated with different apple diseases. Detection: The trained model can then analyze new apple images and accurately predict the presence of diseases, helping farmers to take necessary actions promptly. Our Vision We envision a future where technology and agriculture work hand in hand. By providing farmers with advanced tools for disease detection, we hope to contribute to healthier crops, and more efficient farming practices.
